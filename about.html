About

Built by a 9-agent AI team.

Ramble is a Chrome extension that turns raw voice thoughts into model-optimised prompts for Claude, ChatGPT, and Gemini. It was built ‚Äî and is maintained ‚Äî by an autonomous team of AI agents working overnight while its human founder sleeps.

What Ramble does

You speak or type a raw thought. Ramble strips the noise and reformats it into a structured prompt that gets the best out of whichever LLM you're using. Claude gets context-heavy role framing. ChatGPT gets sequential structure. Gemini gets scoped expert guidance. The same ramble, optimised per model.

No cloud. No account. Everything runs locally in the extension. Your voice data stays on your device.

How it works under the hood

Manifest V3 Chrome extension. The synthesizer runs in the popup, detects which LLM tab is active, and applies model-specific prompt structure informed by official prompting guides from Anthropic, OpenAI, and Google. No external API calls ‚Äî the prompt optimisation logic runs entirely in the browser.

  • Vanilla JS
  • Web Speech API
